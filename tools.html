🛡️ Build Your Personal Privacy Toolkit

The best privacy toolkit is one you'll actually use. Start with essentials and add tools based on your needs:

Essential Tools (Start Here)

  • Password Manager: Choose one like Bitwarden, 1Password, or KeePass for unique strong passwords
  • VPN: Use for public Wi-Fi and to hide your IP from ISPs
  • Encrypted Messaging: Signal for daily messaging with friends and colleagues

Advanced Tools (Add When Ready)

  • Disk Encryption: VeraCrypt for full disk encryption of sensitive files
  • Cloud Encryption: ProtonDrive or Tresorit for encrypted cloud backup
  • Email Encryption: ProtonMail for sensitive business communications
  • File Encryption: GPG/PGP for sharing encrypted files

Best Practices

  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
  • Use unique passwords generated by your password manager
  • Keep all software and operating systems updated
  • Be cautious of phishing emails and suspicious links
  • Review app permissions and disable unnecessary ones

Ready to improve your digital privacy?

Start with one tool from our directory. We recommend beginning with a password manager and a VPN, then adding encryption tools as needed.
